About Me

Hello! Welcome to my parlour of technical and artistic fenangling of powers barely understood by most of humankind!

I earned First Class Honours in Computing & Games Development at the University of Plymouth, which I'm particularly proud of since I chose to wrangle Unreal Engine 5 and all it's quirks for my final year project. And continue to wrangle when inspiration strikes me giving me a potential concussion.

Born and raised in South Africa, and currently based in the UK after studying here. I've always had a love for games and sound, and so I'm cutting a path that lets me explore and create these for the medium I feel has given me so much.

Having created multiple small games projects throughout university with both Unreal Engine and Unity, I'm using the time I have now to expand on a few of those ideas while digging deeper into Audio Programming.

In terms of audio, Cubase and Audacity have been my poisons of choice for the better part of 5 years to create sound effects and music, but I've recently started learning Reaper for...vindictive reasons.

Creating models and sprites is a fun creative release and has the added bonus of being usable in projects I work on! I'm currently using a combination of Blender, GIMP, Krita, and DaVinci Resolve.
