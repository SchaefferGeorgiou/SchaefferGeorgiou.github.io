About Me


Hello! Welcome to my parlour of technical and artistic fenangling of powers barely understood by most of humankind!

I am a problem solver. Most of the problems come in the form of code. But I pride myself on being able to adapt and use universal skills in everything I do.

Like this website! I had no official training in HTML or CSS (which probably shows) BUT IT EXISTS! And you can find more detailed descriptions of what I've learned by checking out my projects below.

I'm originally from South Africa, I've always had a love for games and sound, so I decided I thought I'd follow a path that involves both!

I earned First Class Honours in Computing & Games Development at the University of Plymouth, but I was too explorative for my own good and kept adding new software like Unreal and OpenAL onto already very challenging modules.

Cubase and Audacity have plagued and liberated me (respectively) for the better part of 5 years to create sound effects and music, but I've recently started learning Reaper for...vindictive reasons.

Having created multiple small games projects throughout university with both Unreal Engine and Unity, I'm trying to expand on a few of those ideas while digging deeper into Audio Programming.

Creating simple models and sprites is a fun creative release using a combination of Blender, GIMP, and Krita to create assets used in all projects I'm involved in.
