About Me

Hello! Welcome to my parlour of technical and artistic fenangling of powers barely understood by most of humankind!

I am a problem solver. Most of the problems come in the form of code. But I pride myself on being able to adapt and use transferable skills in everything I do.

Like this website! I had only vague ideas of what HTML or CSS are (which probably shows) BUT IT EXISTS! And you can find more detailed descriptions of what I've learned by checking out my projects below.

I'm originally from South Africa, and currently based in the UK. I've always had a love for games and sound, so I thought I'd follow a path that involves both!

I earned First Class Honours in Computing & Games Development at the University of Plymouth, which I'm particularly proud of due to me taking on far more "out of spec" challenges than I probably should've...

In terms of audio, Cubase and Audacity have plagued and liberated me (respectively) for the better part of 5 years to create sound effects and music, but I've recently started learning Reaper for...vindictive reasons.

Having created multiple small games projects throughout university with both Unreal Engine and Unity, I'm using the time I have now to expand on a few of those ideas while digging deeper into Audio Programming.

Creating models and sprites is a fun creative release and has the added bonus of being usable in projects I work on! I'm currently a combination of Blender, GIMP, Krita, and DaVinci Resolve.
