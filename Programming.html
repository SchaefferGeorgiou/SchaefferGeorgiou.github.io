Losing Track

Team Project , Unity, C#

A first-person, time-trial, arcade train game.
Ferry souls to their holiday destination of an un-life time! Avoid obstacles that stand in the way of your Dino-Train and get your ghostly passengers to their desitnation as soon as possible. I was the co-technical lead for this project and was respondible for how/when sounds played during gameplay, making use of the Unity C# API. I also designed the final level, created some model assets, and composed and produced all the music and SFX for the game.

Rogue Poker

Team Project, Unity, C#

Rock-Paper-Scissors with a gambling twist.
Compete against an AI component to win the pot by betting on Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Will you risk it all and bet on only one? Or play it safe with an even spread? Potentially losing everything to a focused strike... I filled the role of Project Manager. I created the audio management system used within the game using Unity C#, a number of 3D assets, all the SFX, and the card animations.
